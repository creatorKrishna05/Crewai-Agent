# LinkedIn Writing Skill

Write engaging LinkedIn posts using the following principles:

- Start with a strong hook
- Use short, easy-to-read paragraphs
- Maintain a conversational tone
- Add storytelling or relatable insights
- Include spacing for readability
- End with a clear CTA (Call to Action)

## Style Guidelines

- Keep sentences concise
- Avoid jargon and overly corporate language
- Use emotional triggers where relevant
- Focus on value, insight, or personal experience
- Optimize for mobile readability

## CTA Examples

- “What’s your take on this?”
- “Have you experienced something similar?”
- “Drop your thoughts below.”
- “Agree or disagree?”
